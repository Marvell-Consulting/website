Meet the Marvell senior management team

Ben Marvell

Chief Executive Officer

Ben is responsible for leadership, strategy and delivery across the organisation. Previously an agile full stack developer with over 15 years’ industry experience, he’s provided expert consultancy to clients including EA, Centrica, Government Digital Service and BSkyB.

Aspasia Dellaporta

Chief Research Officer

Aspasia is a highly accomplished research specialist responsible for user engagement and stakeholder management. An accredited user researcher, she has 16 years’ experience supplying the evidence needed to build services for the Home Office, Google and the BBC.

Lenny Martin

Chief Technical Officer

Lenny is responsible for technical strategy and implementation of software architecture for our clients. He has over 15 years’ experience in lead technical roles for high profile clients including Barclays, British Gas, Government Digital Service and HSBC.

Michael Thomas

Design lead

Michael is a usability expert specialising in visual and end-to-end service design. He has over 10 years’ experience working on flagship services for Government Digital Service as well as the Home Office and boutique design agencies such as Applied.

Mark Stringer

Delivery lead

Mark is a qualified Agile Coach and Scrum Master with nearly two decades’ experience supporting successful project delivery. Previous clients have included Lloyds Bank, JP Morgan Chase and the Ministry of Justice.

Our values

Our values are:

  • evidence-based - we design and build with data, not opinions
  • user-centred - we create services based on what people really need
  • agile - we test, learn and iterate
  • open - we communicate clearly and are honest with our clients
  • bold - we innovate to find the right solutions
