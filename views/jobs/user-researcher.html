User Researcher - Permanent

Summary of the work

The user researcher will lead the research activities in the agile development lifecycle from discovery through to the live phase of a digital service.

Start date

October 2019

Expected contract length

Permanent position

Location

London Bridge area with some travel to other locations in the UK.

Working arrangements

The user researcher must be available to be onsite at least 4 days a week.

Security clearance

CTC or SC level is preferable. Support can be provided to gain security clearance for the suitable candidate.

Essential skills and experience

  • Proven ability to employ a variety of different research methods based on project needs and the GDS standard
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills
  • Experience of hypothesis-driven design
  • Ability to develop user research recommendations based on qualitative and quantitative evidence
  • Delivering timely research feedback to the team to compliment sprint cycles
  • Demonstrable ability to work effectively with others, including interaction designers, front-end developers and product and delivery leads
  • Knowledge of accessibility best practice and experience of conducting research with users with access needs
  • Understanding of organisational strategy and objectives. Ability to align user research activities with objectives to create strategic insights that inform policy and proposition

Nice-to-have skills and experience

  • Experience within government services
  • Experience researching with users of complex transactional services

To apply for this job...

Please send a CV to talent@marvell.consulting if you have the relevant skills and experience, and you are interested in this role.
